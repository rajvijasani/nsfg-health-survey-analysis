# NSFG Fertility Intentions Analysis

Analysis of fertility intentions using data from the National Survey of Family Growth (NSFG), a nationally representative U.S. health survey conducted by the CDC.

## Methods: 
Logistic regression to model predictors of fertility intentions, with stratified analysis by gender. Handles complex survey design including sampling weights and stratification.

## Language: 
R

## Files:
- `ProjectScript.R` — data cleaning, modeling, and visualization
- `Report.pdf` — full writeup with results and interpretation
- `Appendix.pdf` — supplementary tables and figures

## Data source: 
National Survey of Family Growth (NSFG), National Center for Health Statistics, CDC
